Musicians

Thierry Fischer

Principal conductor of the São Paulo State Symphony Orchestra

The Swiss conductor is the Principal Guest Conductor of the Seoul Philharmonic (South Korea)

Marin Alsop

Music Director of the São Paulo State Symphony Orchestra

Laureate of the Baltimore Symphony Orchestra, as well as chief conductor of the Vienna Radio Symphony Orchestra

Jin Joo Doh

São Paulo Symphony Orchestra cello player

Born in Seoul, South Korea, Jin Joo Doh studied at the Korean National University's College of Music in Seoul with Young-Sook Yun

Ricardo Bologna

São Paulo Symphony Orchestra timpanist

He founded the Percorso Ensemble, created from the desire of its members to encourage the creation of new works and promote 20th century repertoires

Wagner Polistchuk

São Paulo Symphony Orchestra trombonist

Principal Conductor of the University of São Paulo Symphony Orchestra

Soraya Landim

São Paulo Symphony Orchestra violinist

A violinist since the age of seven, she developed technique and performance in contact with great masters of this instrument such as Claudio Cruz, Dmitry Sitkovetsky and Chaim Taub in Tel Aviv
